Welcome to my portfolio. My name is Michał and I am video editor, multimedia designer and aspiring front-end web developer.

#about-me

I am creative person with a passion for design, technology, and filmmaking. I graduated from University of Jan Kochanowski at the Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences in the IT/ICT and I've been working in the field of graphics design and video editing for 10 years and have had the privilege of collaborating with a variety of clients on projects that range from small startups to large corporations. With my background in design and technical skills, I bring an unique blend of creativity and functionality to every project I work on. I am skilled in using the latest tools and technologies in the industry and I'm always seeking new opportunities to learn and grow in my craft. My portfolio showcases a selection of my work and serves as a demonstration of my skills and experience. Whether you're looking for a multimedia designer or a video editor to bring your vision to life, I'm here to help.

#skills

Video Editing:
I have a strong command of video editing software and techniques, allowing me to edit various types of videos for different platforms. Whether it's for television, YouTube, or other social media, I can take raw footage and transform it into a polished and engaging final product. From trimming and rearranging clips to adding effects, transitions, and music, I have the expertise to create captivating videos.

Animated Ads:
I specialize in creating eye-catching animated ads and banners for social media platforms such as Instagram, Facebook and more. Using my knowledge of motion graphics and animation techniques, I can bring static designs to life, ensuring that your advertisements grab attention and leave a lasting impression.

Graphic Design:
In addition to video editing, I have a strong background in graphic design. I can create visually stunning graphic ads, posters, banners, and GIFs that effectively communicate your message and resonate with your target audience. Whether it's for digital or print media, I can leverage my design skills to create visually striking visuals that drive engagement and conversions.

Web Development:
I also possess the ability to create and code simple websites using HTML5, CSS and JavaScript. With my knowledge of front-end development, I can bring your designs to life on the web. I can create responsive and user-friendly websites that align with your branding and goals.
