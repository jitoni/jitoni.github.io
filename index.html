Hi! Video editing and multimedia design has been my profession for the past ten years now, during which time I have been in touch with a lot of clients, from small business owners to corporations. I continuously try to keep up with new trends to improve my abilities in the craft. Honestly, this is one of the things I enjoy about the most - the constant learning. It also highlights some of the works I am most proud of and gives perspective of the kind of work I do. I will bring your ideas to life by editing videos or designing multimedia. Should you have a project, feel free to contact me and let's see how best I can help you.

Skills

Video Editing:
I will edit for your YouTube channel whether it's gaming, vlog, travelling, podcast, talking head, documentary, long/short content etc. I can also edit for television, social media, advertisements. I am capable of taking raw footage and changing it into final product by trimming, rearranging clips, adding effects, transitions, music, text, subtitles.

Multimedia Design:
In addition to video editing, by using my knowledge of motion graphics I can also create animated ads, intro/outros, banners, lower thirds, posters, cards, jingiels for social media platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, TikTok and more.
